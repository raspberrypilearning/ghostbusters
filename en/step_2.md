--- task ---

Add code to your ghost sprite so that the ghost appears and disappears forever when the green flag is clicked.

![ghost-sprite](images/ghost-sprite.png)

```blocks3
when flag clicked
forever
hide
wait (1) seconds
show
wait (1) seconds
end
```
